Awareness

Actions: Experiences strange noises and events in their home.

Pain Points: Lack of reliable information about paranormal activity.

Consideration

Actions: Searches for local paranormal investigators. Reads reviews and testimonials.

Touchpoints: Ghostbusters website, social media, online forums.

Thoughts & Feelings: "Are these guys legit? Can they really help me?" - Hopeful, but skeptical.

Pain Points: Difficulty in choosing a trustworthy service provider.

Opportunities: Showcase testimonials and case studies to build credibility.

Engagement

Actions: Calls the Ghostbusters hotline. Schedules a consultation.

Touchpoints: Phone call, website contact form.

Thoughts & Feelings: "I hope they can solve my problem quickly." - Anxious, but relieved to have found help.

Pain Points: Long waiting times for a consultation.

Opportunities: Offer a 24/7 emergency response service.

Service

Actions: The Ghostbusters team arrives, investigates, and captures the entity.

Touchpoints: In-person interaction with the team.

Thoughts & Feelings: "Wow, they are so professional and efficient!" - Impressed, relieved, and grateful.

Pain Points: The process can be disruptive and noisy.

Opportunities: Clearly communicate the process and timeline to the client.

Post-Service

Actions: Receives a follow-up call and a certificate of paranormal clearance.

Touchpoints: Phone call, email.

Thoughts & Feelings: "I feel so much safer now. I'll recommend them to everyone." - Happy, satisfied, and safe.

Pain Points: Lingering fears or concerns about future paranormal activity.

Opportunities: Offer preventive monitoring services for peace of mind.
