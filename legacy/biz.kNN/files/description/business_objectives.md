# Business objectives

**description**

"business objectives" refers to the high level goals that a company sets out to achieve in a certain period of time. These objectives are the driving force behind all business decisions, providing a clear direction and focus for the company. They are typically aligned with the company's overall mission and vision, and are used to guide the development of strategies and action plans.

In essence, "business objectives" in business modeling is about setting clear, measurable goals that guide the company's actions and decisions. It is a critical element in business design, providing a clear direction for the company and shaping the development of its strategies and operations.
